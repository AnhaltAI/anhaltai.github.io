abstract: ' We present an algorithm for verb detection of a language in question in a completely unsupervised manner. First, a shallow parser is applied to identify – amongst others – noun and prepositional phrases.  Afterwards, a tag alignment algorithm will reveal fixed points within the structures
which turn out to be verbs.  Results of corresponding experiments are given for English and German corpora employing both supervised and unsupervised algorithms for part-of-speech tagging and syntactic parsing.'
